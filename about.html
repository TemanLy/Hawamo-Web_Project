About Hawamo

The story behind your favorite corner café.

☕ Est. 2019 · Jambi

A Cup of Warmth, Born in Jambi

Hawamo was founded in 2019 by a small group of friends who believed that great coffee and a welcoming space could change how people spend their days. What began as a tiny corner shop in Telanaipura has grown into a beloved local landmark — without ever losing the intimacy that made it special from the start.

The name Hawamo blends the Indonesian words for warmth and moment — hangat and waktu — because that is exactly what we want every visit to feel like: a warm pause in your day.

Hawamo is a place for everyone: students pulling all-nighters, friends catching up after work, remote workers who just need a change of scenery, and anyone who simply wants a well-made cup of coffee in a space that feels like home.

Vision & Mission

🔭 Vision

To be the most beloved community café in Jambi — a place where everyone feels at home, creativity flows freely, and every cup tells a story of quality and care.

🎯 Mission

  • ✅ Serve consistently excellent coffee using responsibly sourced beans.
  • ✅ Create a space that supports work, study, and community connection.
  • ✅ Build a team that leads with kindness and grows with purpose.
  • ✅ Keep our menu fresh, seasonal, and genuinely delicious.
